

1.	Be Nice!

The choice of words and tone should not be disrespectful towards anyone.
A big NO to personal attacks or any kind of harassment!

2.	No Hate Speech!

Hate speech is anything that incites violence or hatred against people based on their gender, race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, disability, age, caste, sexual orientation or any other such attributes.

3.	No Breach of Privacy!

Please do not post anything that belongs to another person, without their explicit consent.

4.	Be Original!

Please post your own creation and avoid copyright infringement.
